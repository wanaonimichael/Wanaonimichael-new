Welcome to the DV Lottery Application Page

The Diversity Visa (DV) Lottery, also known as the Green Card Lottery, is a United States government lottery program for receiving a United States Permanent Resident Card. Each year, the program makes 55,000 immigrant visas available to people from countries with low rates of immigration to the United States.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Be a native of a qualifying country.
  • Meet the education/work experience requirements.
  • Submit an entry during the specified registration period.

How to Apply

Follow these steps to apply for the DV Lottery:

  1. Check if you are eligible.
  2. Complete the online application form.
  3. Submit your application before the deadline.
  4. Keep your confirmation number safe.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the DV Lottery?

The DV Lottery is a program that makes 55,000 immigrant visas available annually.

How do I know if I am eligible?

Eligibility is based on your country of birth and meeting certain educational or work experience requirements.

How can I apply?

You can apply online during the registration period. Make sure to follow all instructions carefully.

What happens after I apply?

After submitting your application, you will receive a confirmation number. Keep this number safe as you will need it to check your application status.

The registration period for the DV Lottery typically opens in October and closes in November. Be sure to check the official website for the exact dates each year.
