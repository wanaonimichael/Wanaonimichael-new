FAQs

What is the DV Lottery?

The DV Lottery, also known as the Diversity Immigrant Visa Program, is a U.S. government program that makes 55,000 permanent resident visas available annually to persons from countries with low rates of immigration to the United States.

How do I apply for the DV Lottery?

Applications for the DV Lottery must be submitted online through the official U.S. Department of State website during the application period. Our foundation offers guidance to help you complete your application correctly.

What happens after I submit my application?

After submitting your application, you will receive a confirmation number. Use this number to check the status of your application on the official DV Lottery website. If you are selected, you will be given further instructions for the next steps.
